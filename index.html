Our Story

A Taste of the Swahili Coast

Born from a love of the sea and generations of family recipes, Pwani Kitchen brings the authentic flavors of the Kenyan coast to your table. Our chefs source the freshest catches daily from local fishermen and pair them with aromatic spices traded along these shores for centuries.
